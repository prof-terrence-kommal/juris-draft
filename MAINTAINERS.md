## Maintainer principles

Juris Draft is maintained as a human-review-first LegalTech project. Contributions should improve clarity, safety, portability, and practical usefulness without presenting generated drafts as legal advice.

Maintainer review focuses on:

- whether a contribution is clear and testable;
- whether examples are fictional and safe to publish;
- whether human-review warnings remain visible;
- whether jurisdiction-specific content is appropriately scoped;
- whether the project remains model-agnostic.
