# Security Policy

Juris Draft generates structured drafting packets for human review. It does not verify legal accuracy, detect all privileged information, or provide legal advice.

## Reporting a vulnerability

Please open a private security advisory or email the maintainer if you discover a vulnerability that could expose sensitive facts, produce unsafe output, or compromise downstream integrations.

## Sensitive information

Do not commit confidential client information, personal data, privileged communications, or live matter facts to examples or tests. Use fictional sample data only.
