# South African Legal Source Assistant

## Jurisdiction

ZA

## Research query

mora interest late payment demand letter

## Purpose

This packet provides non-scraping public legal research links and human-review checks for South African legal research. It does not fetch, summarise, or verify legal authorities automatically.

## Suggested public research sources

### SAFLII

Free public access to South African legal information, including case law and related legal materials.

- Homepage: https://www.saflii.org/
- Research link: https://duckduckgo.com/?q=site%3Asaflii.org%20mora%20interest%20late%20payment%20demand%20letter
- Review note: Check court, date, citation, and whether the judgment is still good law.

### AfricanLII

Open access African legal information platform with legislation, judgments, and legal resources across jurisdictions.

- Homepage: https://africanlii.org/
- Research link: https://duckduckgo.com/?q=site%3Aafricanlii.org%20mora%20interest%20late%20payment%20demand%20letter
- Review note: Confirm jurisdiction, source collection, document date, and whether the result is primary or secondary material.

### LawLibrary

Public legal information platform with South African judgments and legal research materials.

- Homepage: https://lawlibrary.org.za/
- Research link: https://duckduckgo.com/?q=site%3Alawlibrary.org.za%20mora%20interest%20late%20payment%20demand%20letter
- Review note: Verify the court, neutral citation, judgment date, and any subsequent treatment before relying on a result.

## Human review checklist

- [ ] Confirm the source is public and appropriate for legal research.
- [ ] Check the court, tribunal, authority level, and jurisdiction.
- [ ] Check the judgment, legislation, or document date.
- [ ] Confirm whether the source is binding, persuasive, historical, amended, repealed, or otherwise limited.
- [ ] Check for later cases, amendments, appeals, or contrary authority.
- [ ] Do not treat generated drafting text or source links as legal advice.
- [ ] Have a qualified legal professional review any legal conclusion before use.

## Responsible use note

This source-assist packet is not legal advice. It is a research starting point for a human reviewer. Always verify the currentness, authority, and legal relevance of any source before relying on it.
