### Clause Redraft Structure

1. Restate the commercial purpose of the clause.
2. Identify ambiguity, imbalance, or missing terms.
3. Produce a clean redraft.
4. Add drafting notes explaining what changed and why.
5. Include optional alternative language if the risk position is uncertain.

### Drafting Controls

- Preserve defined terms supplied by the user.
- Do not change risk allocation without stating the effect.
- Flag points requiring negotiation or local legal advice.
