### Demand Letter Structure

1. Identify the sender, recipient, and legal capacity of each party.
2. Summarise the relevant background facts chronologically.
3. State the obligation, breach, or unresolved issue in neutral terms.
4. Set out the requested remedy or performance.
5. Include a reasonable response deadline.
6. Reserve rights without making unsupported threats.
7. End with a clear next step and contact channel.

### Drafting Controls

- Do not cite cases or statutes unless supplied in the facts or jurisdiction profile.
- Mark uncertain facts as assumptions.
- Keep the draft suitable for legal review before dispatch.
