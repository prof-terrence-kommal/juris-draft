### Settlement Proposal Structure

1. Open without prejudice or settlement-protected language only where appropriate for the jurisdiction.
2. Identify the dispute and parties.
3. Summarise the proposal in practical terms.
4. State what each party will do.
5. Include payment, timing, confidentiality, and release placeholders.
6. Explain that the proposal is subject to written agreement and legal review.

### Drafting Controls

- Keep admissions separate from settlement language.
- Avoid final release wording unless the user has supplied instructions.
- Flag tax, regulatory, and authority-to-settle issues.
